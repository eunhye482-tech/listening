2. 대화를 듣고, 남자의 의견으로 가장 적절한 것을 고르시오.

① 텀블러는 사용하고 난 후 바로 씻어야 한다.
② 인체에 안전한 소재의 텀블러를 구매해야 한다.
③ 텀블러는 완전히 마를 때까지 뚜껑을 열어 두어야 한다.
④ 텀블러는 음료 종류에 따라 세척 방법을 달리해야 한다.
⑤ 환경 보호를 위해 일회용 컵 대신 텀블러를 사용해야 한다.
M: Grace, did you just wash your tumbler and close the lid right away?
W: Yeah, I always do that. Is there something wrong?
M: Hmm, that's not a good idea. You really shouldn't close the lid before the tumbler is completely dry.
W: Really? I didn't know that was a problem.
M: If there's moisture left inside the tumbler, harmful bacteria can grow.
W: Oh no. that sounds pretty serious.
M: Yeah, and it can also trap bad smells inside the tumbler over time.
W: Oh, that explains the weird smell I noticed before.
M: Exactly. So always leave your tumbler open until it's fully dry.
W: Got it. I'll make sure to dry it properly from now on. Thanks!
