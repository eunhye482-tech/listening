7. 대화를 듣고, 남자가 플로깅 행사에 갈 수 없는 이유를 고르시오.

① 코딩 수업이 있어서
② 환경 캠페인 활동을 해야 해서
③ 여동생의 피아노 독주회에 가야 해서
④ 업사이클링 축제에 참여해야 해서
⑤ 가족여행 날짜와 겹쳐서
W: Hey, David! How was the upcycling festival yesterday?
M: Hi, Rachel. It was pretty cool. They had lots of upcycled items.
W: You've been really into environmental issues lately. I heard there's a plogging event this Saturday. Do you want to go?
M: I'd love to join, but I'm afraid I can't.
W: Do you still go to coding class on Saturdays?
M: I used to, but the course ended last weekend.
W: Oh, right. Then are you going to the family trip you mentioned?
M: No, that's next weekend. I'm looking forward to it.
W: I see. So why can't you come?
M: My little sister's piano recital is on the same day, and I promised to go.
W: Oh, that's special!
M: Yeah. She's been practicing for months, so I really want to support her.
