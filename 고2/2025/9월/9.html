M: Hello, students! The 2025 Brain Competition is coming up! This is an annual neuroscience competition for high school students. It'll be held on Saturday, September 13th. For the second round, the students will take an oral exam. Please remember that the usage of electronic devices is not permitted. The winner will get a $500 cash prize.
