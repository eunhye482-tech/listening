7. 대화를 듣고, 여자가 학급 사진 대회에 참가하지 못한 이유를 고르시오.

① 출품 사진의 규격을 맞추지 못해서
② 야외에서 찍은 사진이 없어서
③ 실수로 사진 파일을 삭제해서
④ 신청 마감 기한을 착각해서
⑤ 학급 친구들이 참가를 거부해서
M: Hey, Betty. Did you get the result of the class photo contest?
W: Unfortunately, my class couldn't even take part in the contest.
M: No way! What happened? Your classmates love taking photos.
W: Yeah, we often take pictures together.
M: Then you could have submitted one of your existing photos. Was there any unexpected problem like the size requirement of the photo?
W: No, the size met the requirements. And we were okay with the fact that we should submit a photo taken outdoors.
M: Hmm... Then what was the problem? You didn't accidentally delete the photo for the contest, did you?
W: No. The truth is I thought the deadline was 17th but it was 12th.
M: Oh, no. I'm sorry to hear that. But don't beat yourself up about it too much. Everyone makes mistakes sometimes.
W: Thanks. But I still feel so bad about it.
