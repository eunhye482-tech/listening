W: Mr. Johnson, we're almost done with the major decisions about the interior design of your house.
Now you just need to choose the color for your living room wall paper.
M: That's right. I've seen the color samples you've sent, but I can't decide which one to choose. Do you have any advice?
W: Well, there's one important thing you need to consider when you choose a wall color.
M: One important thing? What is it?
W: If you go with light colors, they will make the space look larger and more open.
M: Interesting. So, dark colors make a space appear smaller?
W: Exactly. This is because light colors reflect natural light and make a space appear larger to the eyes.
M: Hmm... Since my living room is a bit small, I'll go with white.
W: That's a great choice! It's always important to consider the size of the space when choosing a wall paper color.
A light color like white will definitely create a sense of openness in your living room.
M: Thank you for helping me.
