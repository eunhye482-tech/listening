M: Ms. Anderson, are we all set for the culture exchange program with the French students?
W: It’s going well, Mr. Grant. Do you want to go over what we’ve done so far?
M: Sure. I’ve been teaching our students some basic French greetings, and they’ve been practicing their welcome speech.
W: That sounds nice. Your efforts will definitely help the exchange students feel welcome. I’ve prepared all the traditional craft materials for them.
M: It would be great if the students could work in pairs for the traditional crafts session.
W: Exactly. I’ve already made a list of pairs. I paired one student from our school with a student from France.
M: Good. And I’ve ordered keyrings to use as souvenirs. They should arrive this afternoon.
W: Let me know if you need any help.
M: Actually, there is one thing. Could you wrap the keyrings when they arrive? I think I won’t have time for that.
W: Of course, I’d be happy to help you with that.
